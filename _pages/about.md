I am a PhD Researcher in Political Science at the [European University Institute](https://www.eui.eu/) (EUI) in Fiesole, Italy, where I am supervised by [Professor Filip Kostelka](https://fkostelka.eu/). My research sits at the intersection of **polarization** and **political support**, examining how different types of polarization — affective and ideological — affect democratic legitimacy, and under which conditions they are harmful or beneficial for democracies.

Before joining the EUI, I worked as a teaching and research assistant at [Radboud University](https://www.ru.nl/) in Nijmegen, where I also completed my MSc in Comparative Politics and BSc in Political Science. I additionally hold a BA in Music Management from [HKU University of the Arts](https://www.hku.nl/), Utrecht.

**Research Interests**

*Substantive:* democratic legitimacy, political support, polarization (ideological and affective), representation, partisanship, perceptions of electoral integrity, political behavior.

*Methodological:* causal inference, research design, survey experiments, panel data analysis, public opinion research, semi-structured interviews, text-coding.
