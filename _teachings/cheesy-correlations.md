Cheesy Correlations and Causality is a teaching module for social science courses. The data is based on a fictional cheese store and can be used for all analyses, from bachelor to master level. The dataset addresses the impact of COVID-19 and the farmer protests, and contains many hooks and side quests to keep students engaged. It comes with a teaching manual that describes the hooks and hypotheses, and also includes code for R, Stata, and SPSS.
