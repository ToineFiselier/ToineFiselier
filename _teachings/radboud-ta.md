During my time as a Teaching and Research Assistant at Radboud University (September 2020 – July 2022), I taught the following courses at the Department of Political Science:

- **Political Science Research Methods** — Introductory methods course covering research design, data collection, and quantitative and qualitative analysis.
- **Project 1: Explanations for Political Attitudes and Behavior in the Netherlands** — Research project course in which students design and execute an original research project on Dutch political attitudes and behavior.
- **Project 3: Comparative Governance** — Advanced research project course focused on comparative analysis of governance across different political systems.
- **Sociology, Philosophy, and Ethics of Research** — Course on the philosophical foundations of social science research, covering epistemology, research ethics, and the sociology of knowledge.
- **Bachelor Thesis Supervision** — Individual supervision of students conducting their final thesis research.

In addition to teaching, I assisted in writing policy reports for the **Dutch Electoral Council** and the **Netherlands Institute for Social Research (SCP)**.
